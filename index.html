About Moonlit

Moonlit tracks the real-time phase of the moon based on your local date and time. Every phase you see, the shadow angle, the illumination percentage, the crescent direction, is calculated mathematically using the lunar synodic cycle of 29.53 days, not drawn from a photo library.

The moon's position in the zodiac is approximated from its ecliptic longitude, updated daily. Named full moons follow traditional folklore names tied to the month and season.
